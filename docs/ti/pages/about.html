About

Slice of life meets absurdist fantasy: a webcomic.

Tiny Stripz is a webcomic about the quirky adventures of Elaine, her cousins Fred and Amanda, and her sons Alex and Max. It started on 2018-04-22. It is drawn completely by me, but sometimes gets feedback/inspiration from friends and family.

Tiny Stripz is half gag-a-day, half long storylines. I have a fondness for destroying walls and ceilings in my comic strips.

Schedule

Saturdays, sometimes with makeups.
